## Section A — For Hayley

### Question A1
**Type:** Long answer
**Required:** Yes
**Question:** What is the thinking behind the EDEN umbrella structure?
**Description:** In your own words — what is the difference between the four sub-brands and who is each one for? No need to be polished. Just tell me how you think about it.

### Question A2
**Type:** Long answer
**Required:** Yes
**Question:** How would you describe the experience of each sub-brand?
**Description:** If a patient walks in for a sports physio appointment and another walks in for a menopause consultation and another comes for a Reformer Pilates class — how would you describe what each of them experiences?

### Question A3
**Type:** Multiple choice (single select)
**Required:** Yes
**Question:** Which sub-brand is your priority right now — the one you are most actively building or promoting?
**Options:**
- EDEN GP
- Eden Sports Clinic
- Eden Mind Body Spirit
- Eden Home/Away

### Question A4
**Type:** Long answer
**Required:** Yes
**Question:** What are the three most important things a first-time visitor should understand within 30 seconds of landing on the homepage?
**Description:** Think about someone who has never heard of Eden before. What do you most need them to know?

### Question A5
**Type:** Long answer
**Required:** Yes
**Question:** Testimonials — which should stay, which should go, and do you have new ones to add?
**Description:** You can paste new testimonials directly here (quote + name). For removing, just name or describe the ones you want gone.

### Question A6
**Type:** Long answer
**Required:** No
**Question:** Is there anything on the current site you want to keep exactly as it is?
**Description:** Copy, sections, functionality — anything you do not want us to change.

### Question A7
**Type:** Long answer
**Required:** Yes
**Question:** Home/Away — what treatments and services will this sub-brand include?
**Description:** Any pricing you can share now is helpful. If it is not finalised, share what you have and we can work with it.

### Question A8
**Type:** Long answer
**Required:** Yes
**Question:** Opening hours and contact details per sub-brand
**Description:** For the footer and contact section of each page. Format however is easiest — one line per brand is fine. E.g. "Sports Clinic: Mon–Fri 8–6, 07700 123456"

### Question A9
**Type:** Long answer
**Required:** Yes
**Question:** Cliniko — how is the booking set up?
**Description:** Is it one account shared across all sub-brands, or separate per brand? Please share the public booking link so I can test how it works before designing the Book Appointment button.
